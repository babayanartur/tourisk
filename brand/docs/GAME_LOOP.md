# TOURISK GAME LOOP

Version 1.0

---

## Core Loop

Tourisk is built around one simple loop:

1. Open the map
2. See unexplored space
3. Go there in the real world
4. Reveal territory
5. Earn progress
6. Feel curiosity
7. Want to explore again

---

## Why It Works

The player does not play by sitting still.

The player plays by moving through the real world.

Every step can reveal something.

Every place can become part of their story.

---

## The Feeling

Tourisk should make the player feel:

- curious
- present
- rewarded
- connected to the real world
- proud of real exploration

---

## What We Avoid

Tourisk should not become:

- a passive map
- a fake travel checklist
- an addiction loop
- a pay-to-win game
- a social feed without purpose

---

## The Standard

If the loop does not make the player want to explore again,
the loop is not strong enough.
