# 1. The Idea

Tourisk is a mobile game where the real Earth becomes the game world.

Players don't unlock virtual maps.

They uncover the real world by exploring it.

Every real step reveals new territory.

Every journey becomes part of their own story.

We are not building another travel app.

We are building a world people will want to explore.
