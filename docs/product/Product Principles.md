# TOURISK PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the core principles behind every product decision at Tourisk.

Every new feature, design, animation, interaction, game mechanic, or technical decision must strengthen these principles.

If something violates them, it does not belong in Tourisk.

These principles are more important than trends, competitors, or short-term growth.

---

# Principle 1
## Exploration Comes First

Everything begins with exploration.

The purpose of Tourisk is not collecting points.

It is not checking in.

It is not social networking.

It is exploration.

If a feature does not increase the desire to explore,
it should not exist.

---

# Principle 2
## Reality Is The Game

The real world is our game world.

We do not create fictional kingdoms.

We do not replace reality.

Every city.

Every village.

Every forest.

Every mountain.

Every country.

Already exists.

Tourisk simply makes people see it differently.

---

# Principle 3
## Curiosity Is Stronger Than Rewards

People should walk because they are curious.

Not because they are forced.

XP.

Coins.

Achievements.

Badges.

Leaderboards.

These exist only to support curiosity.

Never to replace it.

---

# Principle 4
## No Wrong Routes

Players decide where to go.

Never Tourisk.

No arrows.

No mandatory missions.

No forced path.

Freedom creates exploration.

---

# Principle 5
## Discovery Must Be Earned

Nothing should be revealed for free.

The world opens only through movement.

Walking creates discovery.

Travel creates discovery.

Exploration creates discovery.

Every revealed place has value because the player reached it.

---

# Principle 6
## Every Explorer Has A Unique World

No two maps should ever become identical.

Your world is created by your choices.

Your memories.

Your adventures.

Your life.

Tourisk should celebrate individuality.

---

# Principle 7
## The Map Is Memory

The map is not navigation.

The map is memory.

Years later,
players should open Tourisk and remember moments of their lives.

The map becomes a living diary.

---

# Principle 8
## Simplicity Wins

Every screen should feel effortless.

Every interaction should be understandable.

Complexity is invisible.

Magic is visible.

If something can be simplified,
simplify it.

---

# Principle 9
## Beauty Has Purpose

Every animation.

Every sound.

Every transition.

Every icon.

Every color.

Must support exploration.

Decoration without meaning should be removed.

---

# Principle 10
## Living World

The world should feel alive.

Fog breathes.

Weather changes.

Time changes.

Seasons change.

People move.

Cities evolve.

The world never feels static.

---

# Principle 11
## Respect The Player

Never manipulate.

Never spam.

Never pressure.

Never waste attention.

Players choose how they explore.

Tourisk should inspire,
never force.

---

# Principle 12
## Offline Life Comes First

The goal is not more screen time.

The goal is more real life.

The best Tourisk session is one where the player spends more time looking at the world than looking at the phone.

Technology should disappear.

Reality should become the experience.

---

# Principle 13
## Every Step Matters

No journey is too small.

Walking around your block matters.

Crossing a city matters.

Crossing the planet matters.

Every step becomes part of your story.

---

# Principle 14
## Build For Decades

Never build for today's trend.

Build something people will still love ten years from now.

Every architectural decision.

Every product decision.

Every design decision.

Should make Tourisk stronger for the future.

---

# Product Decision Test

Before implementing any feature, ask:

Does it make people want to explore?

Does it make the world feel more alive?

Does it create curiosity?

Does it respect player freedom?

Will it still make sense in ten years?

If the answer is "No" to any of these questions,
reconsider the feature.

---

# The Tourisk Standard

We do not build another map.

We do not build another travel app.

We do not build another game.

We are building a new way to experience the real world.

Everything we create must bring people one step closer to that vision.
