# TOURISK LIVING FIGURINES SYSTEM

Version 2.0

---

# Purpose

In Tourisk, the player is not represented by a blue GPS dot.

The player is represented by a living collectible figurine.

A figurine is not an avatar.

It is not a skin.

It is not a cosmetic item.

It is a lifelong travel companion.

Every figurine tells a story.

---

# Core Philosophy

A player builds a life.

A figurine builds a history.

Every journey.

Every city.

Every country.

Every discovery.

Every memory.

Becomes part of that figurine forever.

---

# Beginning

Every explorer starts with a single figurine.

♟ Classic Pawn

No player begins stronger than another.

Everyone begins with the same first step.

---

# Figurine Evolution

Players unlock new figurine classes as they explore the world.

Possible evolution:

♟ Pawn

♝ Bishop

♞ Knight

⛵ Boat

♜ Rook

👑 King Figurine

The visual chess evolution remains.

The meaning is exploration.

Not power.

---

# Explorer Rank

Figurines are never unlocked by fixed percentages of the world.

Instead they are unlocked through Explorer Rank.

Explorer Rank grows naturally through exploration.

This allows Tourisk to grow forever without changing the progression system.

Players are never forced to use newer figurines.

"My first companion."

The value comes from memories.

Not rarity.

# Legacy

Some figurines become legends.

Players may retire a figurine into the

Hall of Companions.

Retired figurines remain forever.

Nothing is deleted.

Their stories are preserved.

# Figurines Never Give Power

Figurines never provide:

Extra XP

More Coins

Faster Movement

Hidden Bonuses

Combat Advantages

Tourisk is not about power.

Tourisk is about stories.

---

# The Player Chooses

Every player decides:

Continue an old companion.

Or begin a new journey.

There is no wrong choice.

---

players should smile when opening an old figurine.

Because they remember everything they experienced together.

# Tourisk Philosophy

People do not remember numbers.
