# TOURISK COLLECTION SYSTEM

Version 1.0

Status

Post MVP

---

# Purpose

Tourisk encourages players to explore the world through meaningful collections.

A collection is not a checklist.

It is a journey.

Every completed collection tells part of the explorer's story.

---

# Philosophy

Players never collect for power.

Players collect experiences.

Every completed collection becomes part of their personal history.

# Figurine Evolution

Collections may slightly change the explorer's figurine.

Examples

Churches of Armenia

→ Soft golden halo

Volcanoes

→ Warm glowing cracks

Northern Lights

→ Aurora reflections

Spring Collection

→ Blooming flowers

Ocean Collection

→ Gentle water particles

These effects are subtle.

Elegant.

Never excessive.

Other explorers should notice them only when looking carefully.

Curiosity is more valuable than spectacle.

---

# Personal Choice

Players decide:

Continue improving one figurine.

Or begin a new companion.

Every figurine has its own collection history.
