# TOURISK CONTENT PRINCIPLES

Version 1.0

---

## Purpose

Tourisk does not show places.

Tourisk inspires exploration.

Every place should make the player want to walk a little further.

---

## We Never Write Like Wikipedia

Wrong:

"The Cascade Complex was built..."

Right:

"Walk to the top just before sunset.

The view over Yerevan is unforgettable."

---

## Short

Players are walking.

Nobody reads long articles.

50–120 words maximum.

---

## Inspire Curiosity

Never reveal everything.

Leave something to discover.

---

## Emotion First

Facts are secondary.

Feelings come first.

---

## Reward Exploration

The description should feel like a reward for arriving.

---

## Positive

Tourisk celebrates places.

Not criticism.

---

## Local Feeling

Show why locals love this place.

Not only why tourists visit it.

---

## Every Place Should Answer

Why should I walk here?

---

## End Goal

Every description should make the player want to discover one more place.
